Never use: weight loss · healing · dose · safe for humans · feel better · treatment · before/after · results

Always use: research compound · mechanism · data · studies · protocol · purity · HPLC verified · COA

Do NOT bundle in automated flows: Syringes + bacteriostatic water + peptides as upsell = top FDA warning letter trigger. Keep these separate.
